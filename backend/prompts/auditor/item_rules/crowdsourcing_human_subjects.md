Item 14 (Crowdsourcing and Human Subjects):
  - This item refers specifically to HIRING OR COMPENSATING human workers to collect or label NEW data.
  - REUSING existing, public human-derived datasets (e.g., Ultrafeedback, WildChat, existing RLHF/DPO datasets) DOES NOT count as crowdsourcing.
  - If the authors merely applied algorithms over open-source datasets created by third parties, answer "N/A". ONLY answer "Yes" or "No" if they explicitly conducted new human research or paid workers.
