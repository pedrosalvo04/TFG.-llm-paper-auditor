Item 11 (Safeguards):
  - First, assess whether the released artefact (model, dataset, system) presents HIGH RISK for misuse (e.g., generates harmful content, enables surveillance, synthesises dangerous information, or can be easily weaponised).
  - If HIGH RISK: check for EXPLICIT access restrictions, usage guidelines, or technical guardrails. Releasing a high-risk model under a fully permissive licence (e.g., Apache 2.0) with no restrictions is NOT a safeguard and MUST be flagged as "No".
  - If LOW RISK or FOUNDATIONAL research (no direct path to misuse): answer "N/A". Do not require safeguards for theoretical or low-risk work.
  - Be precise: justify your risk classification with evidence from the paper before rendering your answer.
